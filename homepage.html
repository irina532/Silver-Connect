How to use the website:

We specialize in connecting two types of users: the elderly seeking services and the providers who offer them. Our platform facilitates this connection by offering a seamless service experience.

To begin utilizing our platform, users must first register as either an elderly individual or a service provider based on their role and preferences. Once registered, elderly users can log in to their profiles where they can view available services tailored to their needs. Elderly users can connect with service providers directly through email, exchanging points as a form of currency for services rendered. Providers can confirm service provision by deducting points from the elderly user's account, thus finalizing the transaction. At our core, our website aims to bridge the gap between elderly individuals in need of services and the providers who can fulfill those needs, fostering a community built on mutual assistance and support.

Users:

Our platform serves two primary user groups: Elderly and service providers. Additionally, an admin oversees all procedures to ensure smooth operations.We believe in creating a platform where both the elderly and service providers can thrive, fostering connections and ensuring assistance when needed.

1.Elderly:

Elderly who are aged 60 and above, can benefit from our services aimed at fulfilling their needs. To utilize our platform, registration is required. Through logging in, users can access their profiles, where they'll find a list of available services along with corresponding providers. By initiating contact via email, users can communicate with service providers. Upon confirmation from the provider, the elderly user must allocate points from their stored points to the provider for the rendered service. This process ensures that the elderly can efficiently utilize our website to meet their needs and enhance their quality of life.

2.Service Provider:

Service providers, typically younger prople, offer services tailored to meet the needs of the elderly. Registration and login procedures mirror those for elderly users. However, providers have an additional task: clicking the "Task Completed" button in their profile upon finishing each service. This action allows providers to accumulate specific points for their completed tasks. These points serve as a benefit to providers, as they can later redeem them for services when they reach old age. This unique feature fosters a mutually beneficial relationship between service providers and the elderly, promoting a sense of community and support.

We believe in creating a platform where both the elderly and service providers can thrive, fostering connections and ensuring assistance when needed. Join us in building a supportive community where everyone's needs are met with care and compassion.
